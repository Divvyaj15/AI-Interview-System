## üìä How Your Interview is Analyzed and Scored

The AI Interview System uses a sophisticated multi-step process to analyze your responses and provide comprehensive feedback. Here's exactly how it works:

## üîç **Step 1: Response Analysis Framework**

For each question you answer, the AI evaluates your response using **6 key criteria**:

### 1. **Relevance** (How well you address the question)
- Does your answer directly respond to what was asked?
- Are you staying on topic?
- Do you understand the question's intent?

### 2. **Completeness** (How thorough your answer is)
- Do you cover all aspects of the question?
- Are there important points you missed?
- Is your response comprehensive enough?

### 3. **Structure** (How well-organized your response is)
- Is your answer easy to follow?
- Do you have a logical flow?
- Are your thoughts well-articulated?

### 4. **Specificity** (How detailed and concrete your examples are)
- Do you provide specific examples?
- Are you using concrete details?
- Do you avoid vague statements?

### 5. **Impact** (How you demonstrate measurable results)
- Do you show quantifiable achievements?
- Can you demonstrate outcomes?
- Do you provide evidence of success?

### 6. **Professionalism** (How appropriate and confident your communication is)
- Is your tone professional?
- Are you confident in your delivery?
- Is your communication clear and appropriate?

## üéØ **Step 2: Competency Assessment**

The AI also evaluates you across **6 competency areas**:

1. **Technical Skills** - Role-specific abilities and knowledge
2. **Problem-Solving** - Analytical thinking and solution approaches
3. **Communication** - Interpersonal and presentation skills
4. **Leadership** - Teamwork and management abilities
5. **Cultural Fit** - Values and work style alignment
6. **Growth Mindset** - Adaptability and learning potential

## üìà **Step 3: Scoring System (1-10 Scale)**

Each response receives a score based on this rubric:

| Score | Rating | Description |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| **9-10** | Exceptional | Exceeds expectations, outstanding performance |
| **7-8** | Strong | Meets most requirements effectively |
| **5-6** | Adequate | Satisfactory with some gaps or missed opportunities |
| **3-4** | Below Average | Significant areas for improvement needed |
| **1-2** | Poor | Fails to address the question adequately |
